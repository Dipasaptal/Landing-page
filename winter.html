Winter(Season)

Winter season is the coldest season of the year in India. Winter season can be
characterized by cold wind blow, falling of snow, very low atmospheric temperature,short day, long night, etc.
This season lasts about three months, starts from December and ends in March.refers to the rain and snow of winter in middle and high latitudes. The earliest sunset and latest sunrise dates outside the polar regions differ from the date of the winter solstice and depend on latitude.
During winter in either hemisphere, the lower altitude of the Sun causes the sunlight to hit the Earth at an oblique angle.
